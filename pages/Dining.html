Jonh

Executive Chef

Born and raised in Cannes, France, Olivier Fajol’s culinary passion was ignited by his mother’s home cooking. After following this dream to culinary school, he began his career at the famed Hotel de Crillon in Paris, known for its Michelin stars. In 2004, Chef Fajol joined Four Seasons at the former resort in Terre Blanche, before moving to Mauritius in 2013, then to The St. Regis Bora Bora Resort.
